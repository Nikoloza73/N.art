About N.art

Gold that carries your story

N.art is a handmade jewelry studio based in Kutaisi, Georgia. Every single piece is crafted by hand — shaped, polished, and finished with a jeweler's eye and an artist's soul.

We work exclusively with premium gold to create rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets that feel as unique as the person wearing them. Custom orders are at the heart of what we do.
