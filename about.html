Our Story

Born from a love of beauty & craft

NI Art Studio was founded with a simple but powerful belief: every person deserves to feel beautiful, and every visit to a nail studio should feel like a luxury experience — not just a routine appointment.

What began as a passion for nail art quickly grew into a full studio dedicated to the art and science of nail care. We invested heavily in education, in the finest European materials, and in building a space that feels as beautiful as the work we create.

Today, NI Art Studio is a trusted destination in Tbilisi for those who refuse to compromise on quality. We work with strict sterilization protocols, premium products, and a genuine love for every client who walks through our door.
