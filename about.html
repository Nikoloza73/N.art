Our Story

About N.art

A love of gold, a passion for craft, and a home in the heart of Kutaisi.

How It Began

Gold shaped by hand,
guided by heart

N.art was born in Kutaisi from a deep love of metalwork and a belief that jewelry should be personal — not mass-produced.

We work exclusively with premium gold to ensure that every piece not only looks extraordinary — but lasts a lifetime.

Whether you're choosing something from our collections or commissioning a fully custom design, you'll receive a piece that's truly one of a kind.

Our Values

The principles behind every piece we make and every customer we serve.
