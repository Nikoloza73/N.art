Our Story

About N.art

A love of gold, a passion for craft, and a home in the heart of Kutaisi.

How It Began

Gold shaped by hand,
guided by heart

N.art was born in Kutaisi from a deep love of metalwork and a belief that jewelry should be personal — not mass-produced. Every ring, necklace, and earring we make is shaped by hand, one piece at a time, by someone who genuinely cares about the result.

We work exclusively with premium gold, sourcing only the finest materials to ensure that every piece not only looks extraordinary — but lasts a lifetime. Our studio is a place where craft is taken seriously and no two pieces ever come out exactly the same.

Whether you're choosing something from our collections or commissioning a fully custom design, you'll receive a piece of jewelry that's truly one of a kind — made just for you, in Kutaisi, Georgia.

Our Values

The principles behind every piece we make and every customer we serve.

🔨

True Handcraft

Every piece is shaped, soldered, and finished entirely by hand. No shortcuts, no casting machines — only honest craft.

✨

Premium Gold Only

We use only high-karat gold. The quality of material is as important to us as the quality of the work.

🎨

Design Freedom

We love custom orders. Your idea is the starting point — we turn it into something beautiful and wearable.

🤝

Personal Service

You speak directly with the maker. No middlemen, no generic responses — just honest conversation about your jewelry.

🌿

Longevity

We make jewelry to last generations. Every joint is soldered carefully, every edge polished to perfection.

Sharing Knowledge
